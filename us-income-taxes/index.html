Income Tax Visualizer

This visual is designed to make federal taxes feel easier to understand. As you move through different income levels, filing statuses, retirement contributions, and credits, the chart shows how your taxes change, what shapes those changes, and how those choices affect your take-home pay. The goal is to turn tax brackets and deductions from something abstract into something you can see, compare, and explore.
